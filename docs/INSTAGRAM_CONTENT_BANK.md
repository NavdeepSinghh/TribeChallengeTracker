## Posting Rules

- Keep the call to action app-first: join a challenge, log activity, share progress, submit a win, or invite a friend.
- Use member stories only after Feature Me consent and admin review.
- Do not promise weight loss, body transformation, medical outcomes, or guaranteed results.
- Do not imply paid access, trials, discounts, partner perks, or creator payouts are live until the store/backend/review process is complete.
- Do not scrape or store Instagram DMs, comments, Story replies, poll voters, or private replies.
