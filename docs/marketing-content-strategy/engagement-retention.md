## Engagement and Retention

### First 7 Days

Goal: move new members from curiosity to habit.

Key moments:

- Join a challenge.
- Log first activity.
- See points/streak update.
- Share first proof.
- Invite one accountability partner.
- See the next best action.

Content prompts:

- "Start where you are."
- "One log today counts."
- "Bring one person who will actually check in."
- "Progress proof beats perfect plans."

### Comeback Flows

Goal: normalize restarting.

Use:

- Streak Rescue Prompt Kit.
- Comeback Challenge Invite Kit.
- Midweek Check-In Story Kit.
- Lapsed Member Winback Kit.

Guardrails:

- Do not shame users for missed days.
- Do not create fake urgency.
- Do not spend recovery credits or write logs from copy-only prompts.
- Keep comeback challenges free-first unless paid access is fully validated and clearly labeled.

### Challenge Momentum

Goal: keep active members moving toward completion.

Use:

- Daily prompts from premium pack metadata when unlocked.
- Leaderboards and member stats.
- Weekend Push Story Kit.
- Completion Recap Story Kit.
- Weekly Campaign Review Kit.

Guardrails:

- Leaderboards should motivate without punishing beginners.
- Completion recap copy should reflect actual challenge progress.
- Paid pack copy must not imply a purchase or entitlement unless store validation confirms access.

### Renewal Recovery and Cancellation Feedback

Goal: recover paid value respectfully and learn why members leave.

Use:

- Renewal Recovery Kit.
- Subscription Management Guidance Kit.
- Billing Support Escalation Kit.
- Cancellation Feedback Kit.
- Entitlement Recovery Request and Review Queue.

Guardrails:

- Marketplace cancellation must remain available through Apple/Google rules.
- Do not block cancellation with surveys or offers.
- Do not process refunds, subscription changes, or entitlement writes from client copy.
- Cancellation feedback is learn-only.
