## Audience Lanes

### New Members

Goal: get a first app win quickly.

Primary messages:

- Join this week's challenge.
- Log one session today.
- Bring one accountability partner.
- Share progress when it feels earned.

Best app surfaces:

- Public challenge discovery.
- Invite links with referral attribution.
- Launch cards.
- First-week challenge prompts.
- Progress and win cards.

### Returning Members

Goal: turn missed days into comeback behavior, not shame.

Primary messages:

- Restart with this week's challenge.
- Protect momentum with one simple log.
- Use a comeback prompt instead of waiting for Monday.
- Share the comeback once it is real.

Best app surfaces:

- Streak rescue prompts.
- Comeback challenge invite copy.
- Weekly recap sharing.
- Challenge completion recaps.
- Lapsed member winback prompts.

### Community Contributors

Goal: convert active members into social proof and growth loops.

Primary messages:

- Submit a win for review.
- Invite one accountability partner.
- Celebrate referral progress.
- Help lead the next challenge.

Best app surfaces:

- Feature Me submissions.
- Community Highlights.
- Referral reward ladder.
- Referral Launch Kit.
- Community Ambassador Kit.

### Creators, Coaches, and Partners

Goal: validate demand before paid hosting, sponsor offers, or revenue-share operations.

Primary messages:

- Host a challenge only when the free challenge loop is healthy.
- Review first-party demand before paid terms.
- Keep claims, consent, member data, and support handoff clean.
- Route deeper interest into manual review queues before contracts or payouts.

Best app surfaces:

- Creator / Coach Mode.
- Creator Launch Kit.
- Creator Hosting Application.
- Partner Perk interest capture.
- Partner Campaign Application.
- Partner Perk Claim and Review Queue.
