## Instagram Growth Loops

### Weekly Challenge Campaign

Purpose: create a predictable rhythm that turns Instagram attention into app participation.

Weekly flow:

- Monday: launch the weekly challenge with a Reel, Story frames, and app invite link.
- Tuesday: post a carousel that teaches the habit behind the challenge.
- Wednesday: share a consent-reviewed member win or community highlight.
- Thursday: use a comeback prompt for anyone who has missed a day.
- Friday: post leaderboard, streak, or progress-proof content using aggregate or consent-cleared material.
- Saturday: share founder/admin/creator context and invite one accountability partner.
- Sunday: recap the week and tease the next challenge.

App support:

- Weekly Campaign Scheduler.
- Weekly Campaign Launch Card Kit.
- Weekly Campaign Storyboard Kit.
- Weekly Campaign Preflight Checklist.
- Weekly Campaign Review Kit.
- Instagram Content Calendar.

Success signals:

- Challenge joins.
- First logs after joining.
- Share-card usage.
- Referral joins.
- Feature submissions.
- Completion recaps.

### Share Cards and Progress Proof

Purpose: make member effort visible without exposing private history or making unsupported claims.

Use:

- Win cards after meaningful progress.
- Weekly recap sharing every seven days.
- Challenge launch cards for invite pushes.
- Challenge completion recaps after a real challenge finish.
- Value Proof Story Kit for progress/report-based proof.

Guardrails:

- Use first-party app progress only.
- Do not imply medical results, body transformation promises, or guaranteed outcomes.
- Do not share user content without Feature Me consent and admin review.
- Do not scrape Instagram DMs, comments, Story voters, or private replies.

### Community Highlights

Purpose: turn member stories into trusted social proof.

Operating rules:

- Feature submissions start as pending review.
- Public posts use only consent-cleared, reviewed submissions.
- Captions keep the member's story grounded in effort, accountability, and consistency.
- Private details, health claims, sensitive data, and exaggerated outcomes are removed before posting.
- Featured highlights should route viewers back into the app challenge loop.

### Referral Prompts

Purpose: reward meaningful challenge joins, not link clicks.

Use:

- Referral Launch Kit for next-tier invite copy.
- Referral Story Sprint Kit for inviting one accountability partner.
- Referral Reward Social Proof Kit for celebrating unlocked progress.
- Referral Reward Claim and Review Queue for manual recognition.

Guardrails:

- Count attributed challenge joins only.
- Do not create affiliate payouts, discounts, Pro unlocks, or paid access from referral copy.
- Do not imply a reward is fulfilled until admin review is complete.

### Creator and Admin Prompts

Purpose: help admins and enabled creators publish consistently without turning the app into a social automation tool.

Use:

- Caption Bank Kit.
- DM Keyword Kit.
- Comment Reply Kit.
- Live Q&A Kit.
- FAQ Carousel Kit.
- Collab Invite, Follow-Up, Safety, Recap, and Renewal Kits.

Guardrails:

- Prompts are copy-ready/manual only.
- No auto-posting, auto-replies, scraping, tracking pixels, stored Instagram identities, or off-platform attribution records.
- Paid creator terms stay behind Creator / Coach Mode review and readiness checks.
