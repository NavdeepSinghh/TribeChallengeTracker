## Monetization Pathways

### Subscriptions

Primary offer: Tribe Pro.

Best timing:

- After users understand the free challenge loop.
- After a weekly/monthly report preview shows useful insight.
- After private challenges, custom goals, premium share templates, or creator tools are clearly valuable.

Safe positioning:

- Pro deepens accountability, reporting, personalization, and creator tools.
- Free users can still log, join public challenges, earn core badges, and share progress.

### Premium Challenges

Primary offer: structured challenge packs such as 21-Day Reset, Summer Shred, Beginner Consistency Plan, 30-Day Discipline Challenge, and 75-Day Tribe Mode.

Best timing:

- Seasonal campaign drops.
- Completion of a free challenge.
- Clear demand from challenge joins, referrals, and share activity.

Safe positioning:

- Packs provide structure, prompts, and premium challenge moments.
- They do not promise specific body, health, or medical outcomes.

### Coaching-Style Bundles

Future path: creator-hosted challenges, private challenge links, branded challenge pages, and manual hosted-readiness applications.

Launch requirements:

- Creator profile and hosted challenge metrics.
- Creator terms readiness.
- Payout provider and tax/identity operations.
- Support and refund ownership.
- Store/marketplace alignment if digital access is sold in-app.

### Sponsor and Partner Offers

Future path: sponsor-backed challenges, member perks, and partner campaigns.

Launch requirements:

- First-party partner interest signals.
- Partner fit and disclosure review.
- Destination safety review.
- No third-party data sharing without updated privacy disclosures and implementation review.
- Manual claim/application review before fulfillment promises.

### Paid Community Tiers

Future path: private community access, advanced accountability, creator-led cohorts, or ambassador recognition.

Launch requirements:

- Clear member value beyond free challenge participation.
- Moderation and support plan.
- Subscription or marketplace compliance review.
- No pressure tactics, fake scarcity, or hidden renewal behavior.

### Merch and Event Tie-Ins

Future path: milestone merch, local challenge events, pop-ups, studio partnerships, or seasonal finishers.

Launch requirements:

- Fulfillment ownership.
- Refund/returns policy.
- Safety and liability review for events.
- Clear separation between physical goods/events and in-app digital entitlement claims.
