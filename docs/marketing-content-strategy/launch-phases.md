## Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Release Foundation

Outcome: the app is store-ready and the free growth loop works.

Ship/check:

- Store listing and reviewer notes.
- Privacy, terms, support, and data deletion links.
- First weekly campaign template.
- Invite links and referral attribution.
- Progress/win cards.
- Feature Me submission flow.
- Community Highlights review workflow.
- `npm run release:check:all`.

### Phase 2: Public Free Challenge Push

Outcome: Instagram launches drive real app joins and first logs.

Run:

- 2 to 4 weekly challenge campaigns.
- Manual DM keyword replies.
- Story countdown/start/midweek/weekend/recap sequence.
- Community highlight posts from consent-reviewed submissions.
- Referral sprint prompts.

Measure:

- Joins per campaign.
- First-log rate.
- Challenge completion rate.
- Referral joins.
- Share-card usage.
- Feature submissions and featured approvals.

### Phase 3: Paid Value Validation

Outcome: prove demand before promoting paid access hard.

Run:

- Pro Trial Interest capture.
- Founder Member Offer Kit.
- Pricing Test Kit.
- Revenue Pathway Planner.
- Customer Value Delivery Checklist.
- Paid Launch Decision Gate.

Measure:

- Pro interest reasons.
- Pack interest.
- Creator beta interest.
- Partner perk interest.
- Support readiness.
- Store test evidence coverage.

### Phase 4: Paid Launch

Outcome: paid access is promoted only after store/backend validation works.

Required before launch:

- App Store and Play products exist.
- Firebase Functions receipt validation credentials are configured.
- Sandbox/license test purchases pass.
- Restore/sync flows pass.
- Entitlement writes are verified.
- Support and entitlement recovery flows are ready.
- Store review evidence pack is current.

Promote:

- Tribe Pro subscription.
- Initial premium challenge packs.
- Pro reports/custom goals/private challenges/share templates.

### Phase 5: Creator and Partner Expansion

Outcome: expand distribution without weakening trust.

Run:

- Creator hosting applications.
- Collab pilots.
- Partner perk interest review.
- Sponsor-backed challenge pilots.
- Manual contract/payout/readiness checks.

Do not launch:

- Revenue share without payout/tax/support operations.
- Partner offers without disclosure, destination, and fulfillment review.
- Affiliate or tracking flows without privacy and policy updates.
