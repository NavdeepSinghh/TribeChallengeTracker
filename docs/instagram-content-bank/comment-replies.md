## Comment Replies

### "How do I join?"

```text
Use the challenge link in bio or reply TRIBE and we will send it over. Join, log your first session, and invite one accountability partner.
```

### "Is it beginner friendly?"

```text
Yes. Start with one honest session and build from there. The goal is consistency and accountability, not perfection.
```

### "I missed the start."

```text
You can still come back. Join today, log one session, and let this be your restart point.
```

### "Is this free?"

```text
The core challenge loop is built to stay useful for free: activity logging, public challenges, progress sharing, core badges, and community accountability.
```

### "What do I post?"

```text
Post your real progress: first log, weekly recap, challenge completion, or comeback moment. Tag @risewiththetribe so we can see it.
```

### "Can I be featured?"

```text
Yes. Submit your win through the Feature Me flow in the app. We only feature reviewed submissions with consent.
```

### "Do I need a wearable?"

```text
No. Manual logging works. Wearable sync can help, but the challenge is built around showing up and recording the work.
```
