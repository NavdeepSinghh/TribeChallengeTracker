Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Rise With The Tribe helps members join fitness challenges, log activity, build streaks, earn badges, and share progress. We collect only the information needed to run those features and support your account.

Information We Collect

Account details, profile appearance, optional Instagram handle, challenge participation, activity logs, badges, referrals, feature submissions, purchase entitlement status, and optional health-sync activity imported only after permission is granted.

How We Use Information

We use this information to authenticate users, show progress, run challenges and leaderboards, sync eligible activity, process support requests, verify purchases server-side, and improve the product experience.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Feature submissions are reviewed manually and are only considered for reposting when consent is provided. Store purchases are validated with Apple, Google, and Firebase-backed services as needed.

Your Choices

You can update your profile, disconnect optional health permissions through your device settings, and request account or data deletion from the data deletion page.

Contact

Questions can be sent through the support page.
