Support

We aim to respond to support requests as quickly as possible.

What To Include

Include your account email, device platform, a short description of the issue, and screenshots if they help explain the problem.

Purchase and Restore Help

Future purchases are verified server-side. If an entitlement is missing after a valid store purchase, contact support with the product ID and platform. Refund requests must follow the App Store or Google Play marketplace process.
